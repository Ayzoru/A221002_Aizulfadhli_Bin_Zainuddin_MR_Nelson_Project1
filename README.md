Project Name: CareGo: Student Health & Symptom Tracker

Selected SDG: Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being 

1. The Problem
University students in Malaysia often face high academic pressure, leading to irregular sleep, stress, and physical health symptoms (such as tension headaches or fatigue). These symptoms are frequently ignored or undocumented because most existing health apps are either too complex for daily use or require paid subscriptions. Without a simple way to track these minor occurrences, students often fail to notice declining health trends until they lead to burnout or serious illness.

2. The Impact (Why it matters)
Undocumented health trends lead to a lack of self-awareness and delayed medical intervention. For students, this results in decreased academic performance and long-term mental and physical health issues. By neglecting the "minor" signs, students miss the opportunity for early self-care, which is a core pillar of SDG 3.

3. The Solution (CareGo)
CareGo is a lightweight mobile application designed for "low-friction" health logging. It addresses the problem by:

Encouraging Awareness: Using a dedicated SDG Info screen to educate users on the importance of Good Health and Well-being.

Simplifying Input: Allowing users to quickly log their mood and specific symptoms through an interactive, expandable UI.

Data Persistence: Storing logs in a central ViewModel-based History List, allowing students to visualize their health patterns over time.

Actionable Summary: Providing an immediate summary of their current state to reinforce the habit of daily self-reflection.
